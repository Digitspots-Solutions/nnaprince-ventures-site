About Us

Mr Simple Open Banking Co (operating as Mr Simple API Finance Limited) is a leading Nigerian fintech company headquartered in Ngor Okpala, Imo State. Led by experienced card issuer operations professionals, we specialize in open banking solutions that empower businesses to integrate sophisticated financial services into their platforms. Our API-first approach enables banks, fintechs, and enterprises across Nigeria to launch card programs, process payments, and deliver exceptional financial experiences to their customers. We combine deep industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to bridge the gap between traditional banking and digital innovation.
